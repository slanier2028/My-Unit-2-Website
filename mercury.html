Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and also the smallest planet in the solar system. It's known as one of the four rocky planets in our solar system. This small planet does not have any moons.

Facts

  • Mercury is the fastest planet in the solar system, with it completing an orbit around the sun in just 88 days.

  • A day on Mercury lasts 59 days on Earth.

  • Temperatures on Mercury can go from 800°F to -290°F due to it being close to the sun and it having no atmosphere to contain the heat from the sun at night.

  • Mercury has a very thin atmosphere.

  • Mercury's pole has water-ice.

  • It's one of the two planets in our solar system that doesn't have a moon.

  • Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god named Mercury because Roman Mercury could travel quickly. Mercury travels quickly around the Sun.
