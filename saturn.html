Saturn

Saturn is the 2nd largest planet in our solar system. Saturn is a very popular planet besides Earth due to its large rings. It's one of the two gas giants.

Facts

  • Saturn's rings are made up of chunks of ice and rock. However, it is also suspected that Saturn's rings may have also formed from comets, asteroids, or even shattered moons.

  • A day on Saturn is 10.7 hours and a year on Saturn is 29 Earth years.

  • Although Saturn isn't the only planet to have rings, it's the planet with the biggest rings in the solar system.

  • Saturn has 274 officially recognized moons, making it the planet with the most moons.

  • Saturn's moons, Enceladus and Titan have oceans on their surface that could possibly support life.

  • Saturn is the only planet with an average density that is less than water. If there was a bathtub that could fit Saturn, Saturn would float in it.

  • Saturn is named after the Roman god of agriculture and wealth.
